**Ebb** is a focused, minimal, and uncompromising App & Website Blocker for Android. Built with intention, it strips away the noise so you can reclaim your time and focus.

## 🚀 Downloads

You can always find the latest builds in the **[Releases](https://github.com/joshualebrun/Ebb/releases)** section.

- **Latest Stable Release**: For the most reliable experience, download the APK marked as `Latest`.
- **Pre-Release (Beta)**: For early access to new updates, download the APK marked as `Pre-release`. 

## 🛠️ Configuration & Installation

1. Navigate to the [Releases](https://github.com/joshualebrun/Ebb/releases) page.
2. Under the desired release, download the `Ebb-vX.X.X.apk` file to your Android device.
3. Open the downloaded APK and tap **Install** (you may need to allow "Install from Unknown Sources" in your Android settings).
4. Launch **Ebb** and follow the in-app prompts to grant the necessary Accessibility and Display Over Other Apps permissions.

## 🌟 Features

- **App Blocker**: Block distracting applications instantly.
- **Website Blocker**: Prevent access to distracting domains across supported browsers.
- **Strict Mode**: Lock down your blocks with QR codes, passwords, or time delays so you can't easily cheat during focus periods.
- **Zero Bloat**: No analytics, no AI integrations, no background tracking. Just pure blocking functionality.
